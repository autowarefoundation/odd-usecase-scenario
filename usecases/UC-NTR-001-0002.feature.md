# Feature: Depart while being overtaken

Defines the vehicle's ability to safely depart while a slower vehicle is overtaking.
This allows for efficient driving by avoiding unnecessary waiting when a safe opportunity exists.

## Scenario: Depart during overtake maneuver

Tests the vehicle's decision to depart as a slower vehicle approaches to overtake.
Verifies the Ego car departs without waiting for the Npc to pass.

* Given Ego is in standstill
* And Npc0 is positioned behind Ego, approaching with relatively low velocity Vn
* When Npc0 starts overtaking Ego from distance Dr
* And Ego departs for the destination without waiting for Npc0's overtake maneuver to complete
* Then Ego departs for destination before Npc0's overtake maneuver is over
* And Ego reaches the destination without collision
* And Ego does not decelerate at a high negative acceleration rate
* And Ego does not collide with any of the Npcs
* And Ego completes the maneuver within timeout Tdition
